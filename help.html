Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I reset my password?

    To reset your password, go to the "Account Settings" section and click on "Reset Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

  • How can I add funds to my account?

    To add funds, click on the "Add Funds" button under the "Balance" section and select your preferred payment method.

  • How do I contact customer support?

    If you need assistance, you can contact our support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" section below.
